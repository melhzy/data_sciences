# Scientific Research Writing Tutorial
## 14-Week APA 7 Guide for ANLY699 Applied Project

### Course Overview

This 14-week tutorial provides comprehensive guidance for conducting and writing scientific research following APA 7th edition guidelines. The curriculum is designed to support your capstone analytics project from initial concept to final presentation.

### Learning Objectives

By completing this tutorial series, you will:
- Develop research questions grounded in existing literature
- Design rigorous research methodology appropriate for analytics projects
- Conduct statistical analysis with proper documentation
- Write research papers following APA 7 formatting standards
- Create professional visualizations and tables
- Present research findings effectively

### Week-by-Week Schedule

#### **Phase 1: Foundation (Weeks 1-3)**

**Week 1: Research Foundation**
- Understanding scientific research process
- Components of research papers
- APA 7 overview and basic formatting
- **Resources**: Scientific Research and Methodology.pdf

**Week 2: Literature Review**
- Searching academic databases
- Evaluating source credibility
- Synthesizing research findings
- Building theoretical framework
- **Resources**: APA professional/student sample papers

**Week 3: Research Questions & Hypotheses**
- Formulating research questions
- Developing testable hypotheses
- Defining variables and constructs
- **Resources**: Research Questions.pdf, Develop_a_Research_Question.pdf

#### **Phase 2: Methodology (Weeks 4-6)**

**Week 4: Research Methodology**
- Quantitative research design
- Sampling strategies
- Validity and reliability
- Ethics and IRB considerations

**Week 5: Data Collection**
- Data sources and acquisition
- Survey design and measurement
- Data quality assessment
- Documentation requirements

**Week 6: Statistical Analysis**
- Selecting appropriate statistical tests
- Assumptions and diagnostics
- Effect sizes and power analysis
- **Resources**: Introduction to Research Statistical Analysis.pdf

#### **Phase 3: Results & Analysis (Weeks 7-8)**

**Week 7: Results Section**
- Reporting statistical findings
- Organizing results logically
- What to include vs. what to exclude
- Integrating tables and figures

**Week 8: Discussion Section**
- Interpreting findings
- Linking results to literature
- Addressing limitations
- Implications and future research

#### **Phase 4: Writing Components (Weeks 9-11)**

**Week 9: Abstract & Introduction**
- Writing effective abstracts
- Introduction structure (funnel approach)
- Establishing research significance
- Stating purpose and hypotheses

**Week 10: Citations & References**
- In-text citation formats
- Reference list formatting
- Managing citations in R Markdown
- Avoiding plagiarism
- **Resources**: apa-7-professional-sample-paper-2025-revision.pdf

**Week 11: Figures & Tables**
- APA-compliant figure formatting
- Table design and numbering
- Captions and notes
- R code for publication-ready graphics

#### **Phase 5: Refinement (Weeks 12-14)**

**Week 12: Revision & Editing**
- Self-editing strategies
- Peer review process
- Common APA errors to avoid
- Writing clarity and conciseness
- **Resources**: AI4Research.pdf

**Week 13: Final Draft Assembly**
- Complete paper structure
- Title page and headers
- Appendices
- Final formatting checklist
- **Resources**: apa-7-student-sample-paper-2025-revision.pdf

**Week 14: Research Presentation**
- Creating effective presentations
- Data visualization for audiences
- Oral presentation skills
- Q&A preparation

### Recommended Workflow

1. **Read** the weekly tutorial content
2. **Review** associated PDF resources in APA7 folder
3. **Complete** practical exercises in R Markdown
4. **Apply** concepts to your capstone project
5. **Iterate** based on feedback
